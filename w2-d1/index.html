Introducing California's Greatest Cliffs

What is so unique about California cliffs?

California cliffs are one of a kind in the United States where no cliffs are as manificent and beautiful as them. And Calfomia is the only state that have cliffs that fronts all possible environments: sea, river, mountains and canyons. There are four such individual unique cliffs: West Cliff, North Ciff, East Cliff and South Chill. All offer fantastic overlooking views, recreational areas, camping sites, wildlife watch spots and overnight cabins.

Where are these cliffs?

Located within a 100 mile radius from irvine, Calfornia, they can be reached by roads easily. The closest diff to Irvine is West Cliff, which is the most popular and most visited.
